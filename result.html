Things accumulated during the Human-Machine Communication course

1. Interface design ability:

  • - Apply classroom theory to interface design.
  • - Create a friendly interface, easy to use.
  • - Ensure the principles when designing a website.
  • - Minimize errors.
  • - Website design helps a lot for life.

2. Ability to work in groups:

  • - Although the members initially did not agree, but after one working time, the team was more united, supporting each other throughout too project implementation.
  • - Clear division of work for each member (avoiding the burden team or not doing anything).
  • - The members themselves discussed, set out the task in accordance with the ability to accelerate the project.

3. Ability to listen and understand requirements:

  • - Listen to the teacher's comments.
  • - When being commented on the parts that have not been done well, quickly fix it to suit the requirements of the teacher.
  • - When it was commented that part was fine, continue to promote.
